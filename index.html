The Cheshunt Chiropractic Clinic

The Cheshunt Chiropractic Clinic is one of the largest and longest running clinics of its kind in the Home Counties.

We specialise in back, neck and shoulder pain, headaches and migraines. We do this by diagnosing and treating disorders to the spine and musculoskeletal system.

Our Chiropractic treatment takes place in comfortable, friendly and professional surroundings, with a range of excellent facilities.

These Include:

  • Specialist pain-relief consultant
  • Access to specialist medical treatments*
  • Free car parking

*MRI scans, anti-inflammatory, and epidurals if required

Our chiropractors rely on almost 25 years of designated experience, and strictly work to the highest professional standards.

You'll find us within easy reach of North London, Hertfordshire and Essex. We're located just a few minutes' drive from junction 25 of the A10 and M25.

All patients have access to our large car park free of charge.

At Cheshunt Chiropractic Clinic we offer a three-fold approach to good health
  • Fast pain relief
  • Corrective treatment
  • Long-term preventative care.

We respond to our patient's immediate concerns, focusing on providing fast and effective pain relief, as well as treatment of other symptoms.

After this we offer a program of corrective treatment to address the underlying causes of the condition. Finally, we ensure that our patient’s receive all the support and information they need to prevent the problem from occurring again.
